Sudoku Solver

To solve a puzzle, input it into the left-most box. Type a space where each empty box should go. After finishing each line of the puzzle, hit enter without typing any additional characters, and continue. When your puzzle is fully typed, it should have 9 rows of 9 chaacters and only contain numbers 1-9 and spaces.

Once you have inputed, click 'Confirm Input'. Your puzzle should appear in the middle box. Click 'next' to begin solving. Every time you press 'next' the solver will advance a step in the solution. If you want to skip to the complete solution, click 'Instant Solve'.

Once you are done, if you want to solve another puzzle, refresh the page.
