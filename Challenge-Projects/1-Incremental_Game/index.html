At the outset, workes can crush 1kg of rock into gravel per second. Their productivity is increased greatly with the aid of factory equipment. It will get more and more costly to hire workers until a factory is built, at which point costs will plummet before rising again until the next factory is built.
Stronger Hammer 100

You have been blessed with a mysteriously strong hammer that will enable you to crush a lot of gravel yourself. It will help you get started on your way to becoming "The Gravel Lord."
Build a Factory 10000

Builds a gravel factory that can crush rocks and produce gravel at a high rate. At least 25 workers are required per factory built. Factory prices will rise initially but settle at 15000 once HQ is built.
Build Your Corporate HQ 1000000

This One time investment will build your coporate Headquarters, from which you will gain the ability to improve the efficiency of your business. HQ unlocks upgrades for your workers and factories, which are now on an organized network. It also unlcoks new buildings including a scientific research center and the ability to contract other businesses. At least 5 factories are required before you can build it.
